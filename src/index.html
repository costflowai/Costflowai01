How accurate are these ROM estimates?

Our calculators provide rough order of magnitude (ROM) estimates for planning purposes. They're based on industry-standard methods but actual costs can vary significantly. Always verify with local contractors and get detailed quotes for construction decisions.

Do I need to create an account to use the calculators?

No account required. All calculators work immediately without registration, passwords, or personal information. Just visit a calculator page and start entering your project details.

What types of projects can I estimate?

We have calculators for concrete work, residential projects, commercial tenant improvements, ROI analysis, and more. Each calculator is designed for specific project types with relevant inputs and outputs.

Can I trust these estimates for bidding?

No - these are ROM estimates only. Never use for final bidding or construction decisions. They're helpful for initial planning, but always get detailed quotes from licensed contractors for actual projects.
