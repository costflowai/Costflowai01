Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

Simple Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to operate this site. We do not sell personal data. Calculator inputs are processed in your browser and not stored on our servers.

Information We Collect

Calculator Data

  • Calculator inputs: Project dimensions, material selections, and other calculation parameters are processed in your browser only and are not stored on our servers by default.
  • Export data: If you export calculation results (PDF, CSV), the data is processed locally on your device.

Analytics Data

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. This may include anonymized IP addresses, browser type, pages viewed, and time spent on site.
  • Usage patterns: We track which calculators are most popular to improve our services.

Technical Data

  • Server logs: Standard web server logs including IP addresses, user agents, and request timestamps.
  • Error logs: Technical error information to maintain site functionality.

How We Use Information

  • Provide services: To operate our calculators and website functionality
  • Improve services: To understand usage patterns and improve our tools
  • Technical maintenance: To diagnose technical issues and maintain site performance
  • Communication: To respond to support requests (if you contact us)

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party services like Google Analytics (subject to their privacy policies)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition

Data Security

  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Calculator data is processed client-side when possible
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect stored data
  • No payment information is collected or stored

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics cookies in your browser
  • Data portability: Request your data in a portable format

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies to understand site usage
  • Functionality: To remember your calculator settings during your session
  • Performance: To optimize site loading and performance

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Netlify: Web hosting subject to Netlify's Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
